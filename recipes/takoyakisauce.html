Takoyaki Sauce

Description

Takoyaki Sauce:
A sweet sauce, primarily used to cover fried octopus or use as a dip for octopus dumplings. If it is too sweet for your taste, reduce the amount of honey.

Ingredients

1 servings

  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon ketchup

Steps

  1. Stir together oyster sauce, Worcestershire sauce, honey, and ketchup until well combined.
